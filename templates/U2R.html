Precautions

Regular Patching and Updates Ensure that all software, including operating systems and applications, are regularly updated with the latest security patches to fix vulnerabilities.
Network Segmentation Divide the network into smaller segments to contain potential breaches and limit the ability of attackers to move laterally.
Privileged Access Management (PAM) Use PAM tools to monitor, control, and manage privileged accounts to prevent unauthorized users from gaining root access.
Least Privilege Principle Limit user access to only what they need to perform their job.
Intrusion Detection and Prevention Systems (IDPS) Employ robust IDPS solutions that can detect unusual behavior or unauthorized access attempts in real time and take action to prevent them.
