Precautions

Firewalls Implement firewalls to monitor and control incoming and outgoing network traffic. Configure them to block unauthorized access attempts and filter out suspicious packets.
Regular Network Scans Conduct routine vulnerability scans and audits to identify weaknesses in the system. Address and patch these vulnerabilities promptly.
Encryption Encrypt sensitive data during transmission and storage to prevent unauthorized access even if intercepted.
Monitoring and Logging Continuously monitor network activities and maintain detailed logs. Analyze these logs for any abnormal behavior or potential signs of a probe attack.
Strong Authentication and Access Control Enforce strong password policies, implement multi-factor authentication (MFA), and limit access privileges based on the principle of least privilege.
